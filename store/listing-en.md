## Description (4000 chars max)

Steel Darts Pro is a free score tracking and practice app for steel darts players.

GAME MODES
- Count-Up (8 rounds) — rank system & personal best tracking
- 01 Game (301/501/701/901) — solo, 2-player, or vs CPU
- Cricket — 2-player & CPU matches with MPR stats
- SIM 501 — CPU battle simulation with 5 difficulty levels

TRAINING
- Checkout Quiz — practice arrangement patterns for finishing
- Weakness Drill — auto-detects your weak scores for focused practice
- 5 difficulty levels — beginner to pro

STATISTICS & ANALYSIS
- 3-dart average, best round, total score
- 100+/140+/180 counts
- Game history with trend graphs
- Cricket MPR analysis per target

MORE FEATURES
- XP & Level system — grow as you play
- Daily Streak — track consecutive play days
- Daily Challenge — fresh goals every day
- Caller voice — realistic darts experience
- Japanese / English support
- Dark theme
- Works offline — no internet required
- Data export / import

100% free. No ads. No signup. Start practicing now!
